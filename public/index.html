Server-Side Processing Pipeline
When you upload a CSV, the server:
① Parses & detects delimiter — comma, semicolon, or tab
② Maps column names — fuzzy-matches 16 standard fields
③ Cleans & validates — filters invalid prices, nulls
④ Computes 18 features — including cyclical month encoding
⑤ Trains OLS regression — normal equations with ridge (λ=0.001)
⑥ Reports R² and RMSE — model quality on your data
⑦ Ready for predictions — all adjustments applied server-side
